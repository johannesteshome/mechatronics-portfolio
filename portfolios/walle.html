Wall-E Machine

Collaborated with Robox Robotics and IA Center to design a highly detailed and functional Wall-E robot. This project involved integrating advanced robotics components, including motorized arms, sensor systems, and interactive features. The design focused on capturing the essence of Wall-E while ensuring seamless functionality and user interaction. By leveraging my expertise in SolidWorks, I successfully created a visually captivating and technically advanced robot that showcases the capabilities of Robox Robotics and IA Center in creating cutting-edge robotic solutions.
